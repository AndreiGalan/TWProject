Why this project?

We chose this project because we are passionate about education, promoting healthy eating habits, and enjoy the technical challenge of creating a fun and educational web app for primary school students.

How does it work?

The game consists of earning points through different play mode: guessing fruits/vegetables or solving mathematical equation, where the variables are fruits/vegetables. The game includes a scoring system, leaderboard, and levels of difficulty.
