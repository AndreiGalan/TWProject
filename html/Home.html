Fruits OnTheWeb

>Welcome to our educational web game, designed specifically for primary school students! Our game is based on data regarding images of vegetables and fruits, providing an interactive learning experience that teaches young students about the importance of a healthy diet and the benefits of consuming fruits and vegetables.

>Our game features a fun and engaging scenario, stakes, and victory/defeat conditions established by our team.
